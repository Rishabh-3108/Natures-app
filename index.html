About Us

Born from a Love of
the Natural World

Nature's Touch was founded on a simple belief: that the waste our forests leave behind can become tomorrow's treasures. We partner with local communities, NGOs and eco-conscious buyers to collect natural debris and transform it into premium, sustainable products.


Our work creates livelihoods, reduces landfill waste, and brings the beauty of nature into everyday life — one product at a time.
