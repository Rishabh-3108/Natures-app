Our Sustainable Process

From fallen tree to finished product — every step guided by environmental responsibility.

1

Collection of Fallen Trees

Our team collaborates with local authorities, NGOs, and community volunteers to identify and collect fallen trees and branches — especially those from storm-damaged or disaster-affected areas. We also receive reports from citizens through our website's Report feature.

Materials collected include fallen branches, leaves, bark, and wood scraps. No healthy trees are ever cut.

NGO PartnershipsCommunity VolunteersZero Deforestation
2

Transportation & Logistics

Collected materials are carefully transported to our central processing facility using eco-efficient vehicles. We batch deliveries to minimize carbon emissions and work with local logistics partners to reduce the supply chain footprint.

Batch LogisticsEmission ReductionLocal Partners
3

Sorting & Segregation

At the facility, materials are sorted by type — wood, leaves, bark, and fibres. Segregation ensures the right materials go into the right products. Metals, plastics and non-organic items are separated out for proper disposal or recycling.

Manual SortingQuality SelectionWaste Segregation
4

Shredding & Pulping

Wood and organic matter are shredded and pulped using our blending method — combining natural materials to create smooth, durable base sheets. We use water-based processing and avoid harmful chemicals, ensuring our pulp is non-toxic and eco-standard compliant.

Blending MethodChemical-FreeEco-Standard Compliant
5

Product Manufacturing

Processed pulp is shaped into sheets, bags, calendars, notebooks, and more. Our artisans combine traditional craftsmanship with modern eco-design to create products that are beautiful, durable, and fully biodegradable. Seed papers are embedded with herb and flower seeds at this stage.

HandcraftedSeed EmbeddingEco InksBiodegradable
6

Quality Check & Packaging

Every product goes through a rigorous quality check for strength, finish, print quality, and eco-standard compliance. Products are then packaged in our own eco-friendly kraft paper packaging — no plastic, no styrofoam.

Quality AssuranceKraft PackagingZero Plastic
7

Delivery & Community Reinvestment

Orders are delivered to your doorstep. A portion of every sale is reinvested into community reforestation efforts and awareness workshops. We also track environmental impact metrics — trees saved, waste diverted, and CO₂ prevented — and publish them transparently.

Eco DeliveryReforestation FundImpact Tracking
Methods We Use

Science Behind Our Craft

🔀 Blending Method

Combining different natural fibres (wood pulp + leaf fibre + bark) in optimal ratios to create smooth, durable eco-sheets with consistent texture and strength.

♻️ Recycling Method

Reusing fallen leaves, bark, and wood scraps through a clean pulping process — minimising waste at every step and maximising material utilisation.

🌱 Seed Embedding

Wildflower, herb, and vegetable seeds are pressed into our plantable paper products during manufacturing, giving the paper a second life after use.

📐 Eco-Standard Compliance

All products are tested against material composition standards, eco-ink regulations, and sheet dimension specifications for market-ready quality.

🧪 Sustainability Analysis

We conduct life-cycle analysis on every product to understand and minimise environmental impact from collection to disposal.

🔬 Testing & QA

Measurement and testing techniques assess product strength, quality, and finish — ensuring every item meets our durability and usability benchmarks.
