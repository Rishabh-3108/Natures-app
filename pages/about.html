About Us

Born from a belief that nature's waste can become tomorrow's wonder.

Our Mission

Turning Fallen Trees
into Treasures

Nature's Touch began with a simple observation: after every storm, fallen trees and leaves lay scattered — burning them only added to pollution. We saw an opportunity to transform this waste into beautiful, functional products.

Today, we work with local communities, NGOs, and eco-conscious businesses to collect natural debris and craft it into premium stationery, packaging, and lifestyle products — all while creating livelihoods and protecting our forests.

Our Journey

A Timeline of Growth

1

Observation & Research

Visited forest areas, spoke to communities, and identified the opportunity in fallen natural debris.

2

First Products

Launched our first line of recycled notebooks and kraft paper bags, testing with local eco stores.

3

Growing Impact

Expanded to seed pencils, plantable calendars, herb kits and combo packs. 5,000+ trees saved.

4

Today & Beyond

Online platform launched. Building a nationwide network of collectors, artisans and eco-buyers.
